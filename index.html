About us

Telluric Offshoots, a proud curator of exquisite Indian handicrafts that blend tradition with contemporary elegance. Our company is a celebration of India's rich cultural heritage, showcasing a diverse range of handmade treasures crafted by skilled artisans across the nation.

We believe in preserving and promoting the time-honoured artistry that has been passed down through generations. Each piece in our collection tells a story, reflecting the vibrant colours, intricate designs, and unparalleled craftsmanship that define Indian handicrafts.

Our commitment to authenticity and quality is evident in every creation we offer. From intricately detailed and meticulously carved artefacts, we bring you a curated selection that captures the essence of India's artistic legacy.

As a socially responsible company, we prioritise fair trade practices, ensuring that our artisans receive the recognition and compensation they deserve for their remarkable skills. By choosing Telluric Offshoots, you not only bring home a piece of India's cultural tapestry but also contribute to the empowerment of skilled craftsmen and women.

Whether you're seeking unique home decor items, exquisite gifts, or timeless accessories, Telluric Offshoots is your gateway to the soulful world of Indian handicrafts. Join us on a journey that transcends borders and brings the magic of Indian craftsmanship into your life.

Welcome to Telluric Offshoots, where tradition meets modernity, and every creation is a testament to the enduring beauty of Indian handicrafts.
